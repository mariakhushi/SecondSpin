Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure the quality of second-hand clothes?

We carefully inspect each item for quality and condition before adding it to our collection. Our team ensures that all pieces meet our high standards for cleanliness and wear.

Do you offer returns or exchanges?

Yes, we offer a 14-day return policy for all items. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange it for store credit.

How often do you update your inventory?

We add new items to our collection daily. Be sure to check back often or sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on our latest arrivals.

Can I sell or donate my clothes to SecondSpin?

We currently don't accept direct donations or purchases from individuals. However, we partner with select thrift stores and vintage shops to source our inventory.
